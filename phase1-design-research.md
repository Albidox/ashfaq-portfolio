## Things To Avoid
- Do not copy exact Dribbble/Behance layouts.
- Do not make it look like a generic AI template.
- Do not use too much 3D.
- Do not use too many particles.
- Do not make every section glow.
- Do not make the project section look fake.
- Do not show completed projects that are not built.
- Do not make the website slow on mobile.
- Do not make it only resume-focused.
- Do not use paid assets.

## Final Recommended Direction
- Final mood direction: Premium dark developer command center with business-first clarity.
- Recommended hero style: Command-center hero with concise backend value proposition, trust marks, and direct CTA.
- Recommended navbar style: Minimal sticky navbar with focused links (Services, Projects, Process, Contact).
- Recommended service card style: Glassmorphism-influenced cards with icon, business outcome, and stack hint.
- Recommended project card style: Proof-oriented cards (problem, solution, stack, result, CTA).
- Recommended animation style: Subtle animated grid/glow, staggered reveal, restrained hover effects.
- Recommended contact CTA style: High-contrast dual CTA + trust microcopy.
- Recommended color palette: Near-black/navy base with cyan + electric blue + selective violet accents.
- Recommended typography direction: Readable modern sans for UI/body with optional mono accent for code flavor.
